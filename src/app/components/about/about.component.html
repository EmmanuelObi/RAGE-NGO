Welcome to R.A.G.E. - Empowering Nigerian Children Through Education

At R.A.G.E. (Rights and Aid Group for Education), we believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their circumstances. With a passionate commitment to transforming lives through learning, we are dedicated to providing free education to one million Nigerian children aged 3 to 17.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Through our innovative programs and tireless advocacy, we strive to break down barriers to education and empower the next generation of leaders. Together, we can create a brighter future for Nigeria by investing in the education of its children.
